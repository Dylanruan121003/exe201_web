Our story

In a bustling city, Vie felt overwhelmed by the daily pressures of work and life. One evening, she stumbled upon a hidden garden glowing with strawberry-shaped flowers. As she reached out, a magical pink cat named Dâu appeared, with a body resembling a strawberry and a playful, comforting aura. Dâu came from Planet Strawberry, a world where everything was sweet, pink, and filled with joy.

Dâu shared stories of spreading warmth and happiness to those who felt stressed or lonely. Inspired by this, Hana created Ichigo Haato, meaning "Strawberry Heart." The brand was built around Dâu’s mission to bring comfort and small moments of joy to modern women through delightful products like plush toys, scented candles, mochi, and strawberry tea—each infused with the magic of Dâu’s world.

"Bringing sweetness and comfort to life's toughest moments, Ichigo Haato is a reminder that even in the busiest days, there’s always room for a little joy."
